Harry loved seeing the wonder on their children’s faces as they looked at the Christmas tree and all it’s decorations. There was wonder on Draco’s face as well, and Harry had to sigh when he thought of his childhood Christmases at Malfoy Manor, those rigid and formal occasions. He decisively did 

not

 think about his own childhood Christmases, those thoughts had no place here. Instead, he lost himself in wonder alongside everyone else, admiring the way their tree looked, how the sparkling lights reflected from the decorations and how it seemed like there was real snow dusting the fir branches.
